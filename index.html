Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our taxi services and booking process.

How do I book a taxi? +
You can book a taxi through our website using the booking form, call our dispatch center at (555) 123-TAXI, or use our mobile app. We recommend booking in advance for guaranteed availability.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and mobile payments including Apple Pay and Google Pay.
Are your drivers licensed and insured? +
Yes, all our drivers are fully licensed, insured, and have undergone comprehensive background checks. They also receive regular safety training and customer service education.
Do you provide airport transfers? +
Absolutely! We offer reliable airport pickup and drop-off services. We monitor flight schedules to adjust for delays and ensure timely arrivals.
Can I cancel or modify my booking? +
Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 30 minutes before the scheduled pickup time without any charges. Contact our customer service for assistance.
Do you offer corporate accounts? +
Yes, we provide corporate transportation solutions with special rates, monthly billing, and dedicated account management. Contact us to set up a corporate account.
